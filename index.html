ABOUT THE INSTITUTION
About St. Xavier's College of Engineering

St. Xavier's College of Engineering, Nagpur, established in 1984, is a premier institution committed to academic excellence, innovation, and all-round development of engineering students. Affiliated to Nagpur University and accredited by NAAC with an 'A' Grade, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across six engineering departments.
